--- layout: post title: 'Day 24: Less Entertainment' date: '2016-03-30T06:41:00.000-04:00' author: Jordan O'Connor tags: - Blog - Vision - Living Intentionally - Daily Blog - Intentional - Help - Leadership - Self Help modified_time: '2016-03-30T06:41:55.731-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1202201332823762457.post-9019128980795059992 blogger_orig_url: http://dailyblog.jordanmoconnor.com/2016/03/day-24-less-entertainment.html --- I can't be focused at work when I am jumping on YouTube or any social media every 30 minutes. 

I'm not sure why I got into the habit of watching YouTube at work. I think it was a combination of a lack of interaction with people, and having my cube be moved to a place that has less people that work with me. 

It's killing my productivity and my motivation to be proactive at work. The only time I get anything done is the day that it's due. 

Most of the things I have to do lately aren't that difficult or time consuming, so I procrastinate until the last minute to get it done.

I also have a hard time talking to people about progress outside of meetings. Something will come up in a meeting and I sometimes get a hint of 'why didn't you tell me that earlier?'. 

These things are making work miserable for me. It makes me not want to go in to work. Just kinda sucks.

All of this is in my control, therefore I can change all of it. I have touted about moving my cube back to where I used to be, but I don't know if that is entirely necessary. 

I should be able to thrive wherever I am. I should be able to be accountable wherever I am. I should be able to be productive wherever I am. It's just that simple.

I can't have excuses about my situation when my path is entirely in my control. Regardless of the progress I make outside of work, I still have to show up at work. I should make the most of it.

I really need to set up a meeting with my engineering manager. It's been too long.

I will be proactive about it and do it today.
