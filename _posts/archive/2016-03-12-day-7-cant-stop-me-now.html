A Whole Week

I don't remember the last time that happened


I'm pretty happy with myself for sticking this out for a whole week already. Its nothing major, but it points out that I can actually accomplish something that I set out to do.

All of this last week's blog posts have been a brain dump to get my priorities straight. They have been mostly critical and focused on a negative aspect of my life. 

I don't want things to always be like that. I am not always going to have the same problems over and over again. I am going to continue to grow.

I think the goal for this blog is to use it as a platform for acknowledging the things I need to work on, creating a written path as to how I will change that thing, making note about the process and then sharing my success after I feel like I have successfully changed that thing.

I also would like to use this blog as a platform to inspire and help other people that want to get their lives in order. I want other people to want to live their life as their best self, not someone who doesn't aspire to their dreams.

I want to live intentionally. I don't want to just let life happen to me. I think that is clear.

I'm going to read some books and do some research on things that can help me do that. I know I will need my wife's help as well. 

Right now I am reading EntreLeadership by Dave Ramsey because I think that I would one day like to own my own company and work for myself. 

I would like to get the book: Living Forward by Michael Hyatt, which talks about living intentionally. 

As always, I will post my progress here. 

This feels good.

Jordan
