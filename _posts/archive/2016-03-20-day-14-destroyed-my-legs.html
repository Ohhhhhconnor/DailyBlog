Today I was working on my half marathon goal. I found out the block that I live on has an almost perfect 2 mile loop. It makes things perfect for calculating pace and distance without bringing up Google maps.

Yesterday I ran 2 miles at a 9:30 min/mile pace. Today, even though I was pretty sore from yesterday, decided to up my pace to a 9:00 min/mile pace for two miles. My quads are killing me.

It's a good thing though, as long as I don't overdo it. The last thing I need to do is injure myself and not achieve my goal. I've gotta crawl before I walk, and walk before I run.

A 9:00 pace is almost overkill if I just want to get under two hours. I think that's a reasonable goal to have. I'd rather be over prepared than under prepared.

We have plenty of time until September though. I'm not too worried at all about making the progress we need to get the time we want.

It's late. Short post tonight.
